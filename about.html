About Us

Our Journey

The Story Behind Ashapura Electricals

Founded in 2002 with a vision to revolutionize the electrical industry, Ashapura Electricals began as a small family business with big dreams. What started as a modest electrical components store has grown into a leading provider of comprehensive electrical solutions.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and an uncompromising focus on customer satisfaction. We've evolved from a traditional electrical shop to a modern, technology-driven company that serves diverse sectors including residential, commercial, and industrial markets.
